Style Guide

This style guide outlines the design principles and guidelines for creating a cohesive and user-friendly interface. It covers various aspects of design, including wireframe, typography, color schemes, and simple mockup. I have divided this style guide into 3 sections: Visceral Design, Behavioral Design, and Reflective Design.

Visceral

Designing for first impressions covering information layout, typography and colour.

Behavioural

Designing for functionality covering key interactive components including forms with a focus on micro-interactions and feedback.

Reflective

Designing for long-term impact covering user journey, trust and credibility.
