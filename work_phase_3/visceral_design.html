Visceral Design

The visceral design of this style guide highlights the emotional and aesthetic impact of the proposed website through its wireframes, typography, and color scheme. The wireframes showcase a clean, intuitive layout that guides user attention naturally. Typography is chosen for clarity and personality, balancing readability with visual charm. The color scheme evokes a consistent mood, using harmonious tones to create a welcoming and engaging experience from the first glance.
